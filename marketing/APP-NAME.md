---

## Decision Rationale

### Why @FashionTONbot?

| Criterion | Rating | Explanation |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **Clarity** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Immediately communicates "fashion" + "TON blockchain" |
| **Memorability** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ | Easy to remember, pronounce, and spell |
| **Brand Alignment** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Perfectly matches project positioning |
| **Searchability** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | "Fashion" + "TON" are key search terms |
| **Uniqueness** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ | Distinctive in the TON ecosystem |
| **Length** | ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ | 13 characters (acceptable, not ideal) |

### Key Strengths

1. **Dual Meaning Recognition**
   - "Fashion TON" = Fashion on TON blockchain
   - "FashionTON" = Play on "fashion town" (fashion community)

2. **SEO/Discoverability**
   - Contains "fashion" (high fashion search volume)
   - Contains "TON" (blockchain ecosystem identifier)
   - Will rank for "fashion crypto," "TON fashion app"

3. **Versatility**
   - Works as bot username (@FashionTONbot)
   - Works as display name (FashionTON Wardrobe)
   - Works as shorthand (FashionTON)
   - Works for future domains (fashionton.app, fashionton.io)

4. **International Appeal**
   - "Fashion" is universally understood
   - "TON" is globally recognized crypto term
   - No translation needed across markets

## Alternative Options Considered

### Option 2: @StyleTONbot

| Aspect | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Pros** | Shorter (11 chars), catchy, modern |
| **Cons** | Less explicit fashion connection; "style" is broader/more ambiguous |
| **Verdict** | ❌ Rejected — Too vague, could be interior design, hair, etc. |

### Option 3: @WardrobeTONbot

| Aspect | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Pros** | Descriptive, clear use case |
| **Cons** | Long (14 chars), harder to type, less catchy |
| **Verdict** | ❌ Rejected — Too long, clunky pronunciation |

### Option 4: @ChicTONbot

| Aspect | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Pros** | Elegant, short (10 chars), sophisticated |
| **Cons** | "Chic" may not resonate with younger demo; less searchable; snobbish connotation |
| **Verdict** | ❌ Rejected — Too exclusive, harder to discover organically |

### Option 5: @FitCheckTONbot

| Aspect | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Pros** | Trendy slang, Gen Z appeal |
| **Cons** | May date quickly; less clear for non-English speakers; slang can exclude |
| **Verdict** | ❌ Rejected — Good for features, not for brand |

### Option 6: @ClosetCoinbot

| Aspect | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Pros** | Alliterative, hints at monetization |
| **Cons** | Loses TON connection; "coin" feels generic crypto; not fashion-forward |
| **Verdict** | ❌ Rejected — Loses key blockchain identifier |
